About Us:

Department of Engineering Chemistry was established at the very inception of the college in 1980. The objectives of the department are to train and orient the engineering entrants towards experimentationand required scientific skills. The well-qualified faculty of the department carry forward various functions/activities that are interwoven with multidisciplinary aspects that bring science to the society.
